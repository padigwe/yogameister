About the founder

Dedicated to the transformative power of yoga

Mark Bell is the heart and soul of Yogameister Yoga Academy. With advanced training from the prestigious Rishikesh Yoga Training School in India, Mark has dedicated his life to sharing the transformative power of yoga.

His classes are a perfect balance of challenge and relaxation, designed to cater to all skill levels. Mark's clear instructions, calming presence and attention to safety create a welcoming environment where every student can practise safely and effectively.
