Welcome to the studio

Yoga classes with Mark Bell in Yarm on Tees

At Yogameister Yoga Academy, Mark teaches studio-based yoga in Yarm on Tees for adults of all ages and abilities. Drawing on his formal yoga teacher training, he offers clear, structured sessions that blend strength-building postures, gentle stretching, breathwork and guided relaxation.

You can expect steady, well-paced classes with individual guidance, never a rushed or intimidating gym-style environment.
